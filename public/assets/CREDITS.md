## Audio — starter set (original/procedural)
- `audio/sfx/*.wav`, `audio/ambient/*.wav`, `audio/music/*.wav` — generated by

  (royalty-free, commercial-OK, not CC0 — see `audio/LICENSE-sonniss.txt`) for the
  marquee naval booms. Update this section when those land.

CC0 means no attribution is required; credit given anyway because these packs
are excellent.
